Rancho Baked Beans

Recipe from Jo Mysicka

Recipe Completely Fills A Dutch Oven (May Slightly Overflow With 2 Packs Of Hotdogs.)

- 2 cups chopped onions
- 1 pound ground beef
- 1 cup ketchup
- 2 tablespoons prepared yellow mustard (like French’s)
- 2 teaspoons cider or red wine vinegar
- 2 x 28 ounce (1 lb 12 oz) cans of pork & beans in tomato sauce (Such as bush's baked beans)
- 1 x 1 pound (15 to 16 oz) can of kidney beans, drained
- 1 package of hot dogs or Polish kielbasa, sliced into bite sized pieces (could be increased to 2 packages) (we most commonly use 20 hotdog packages)

1. Preheat oven to 400°F.

2. In a large dutch oven, brown ground beef and onion in a little olive or vegetable oil over medium-high heat until meat is cooked and onion is soft.
3. Drain off fat.
4. Add the remaining ingredients and stir together.
5. Simmer on the stove for a few minutes to heat up mixture. Put lid on pot and place in oven.

6. Bake at 400°F for 30 to 40 minutes or until hot dog is done (it will look a little puffed).
