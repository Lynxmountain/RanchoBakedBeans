Rancho Baked Beans

Recipe from Jo Mysicka

- 2 cups chopped onions
- 1 pound ground beef
- 1 cup ketchup
- 2 tablespoons prepared yellow mustard (like French’s)
- 2 teaspoons cider or red wine vinegar
- 2 x 28 ounce (1 lb 12 oz) cans of pork & beans in tomato sauce
- 1 x 1 pound (15 to 16 oz) can of kidney beans, drained
- 1 package of hot dogs or Polish kielbasa, sliced into bite sized pieces (could be increased to 2 packages) (we most commonly use 20 hotdog packages)


Preheat oven to 400°F.

In a large dutch oven, brown meat and onion in a little olive or vegetable oil over medium-high heat until meat is cooked and onion is soft. Drain off fat. Add the remaining ingredients and stir together. Simmer on the stove for a few minutes to heat up mixture. Put lid on pot and place in oven.

Bake at 400°F for 30 to 40 minutes or until hot dog is done (it will look a little puffed). Use caution when removing lid – it’s easy to forget the hot pad!
